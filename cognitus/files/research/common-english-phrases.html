Common English Phrases

Here’s a list of common English phrases that you’ll hear or use daily. I’ll break them down by category so it’s easier to remember:

Greetings & Introductions

  • Hello / Hi / Hey
  • Good morning / Good afternoon / Good evening
  • How are you? / How’s it going?
  • Nice to meet you / Pleased to meet you
  • What’s your name? / Where are you from?

Polite Expressions

  • Please / Thank you / You’re welcome
  • Excuse me / I’m sorry / My apologies
  • Could you help me? / Would you mind…?
  • Nice to meet you / It’s a pleasure

Everyday Conversation

  • How’s your day? / How have you been?
  • What do you do? / What’s your job?
  • I like it / I don’t like it
  • I think so / I don’t think so
  • That’s great! / That’s terrible!

Asking & Giving Directions

  • Where is…? / How do I get to…?
  • Turn left / Turn right / Go straight
  • Is it far? / How long does it take?

Shopping / Ordering

  • How much is this? / Can I try this on?
  • I’d like… / Can I have…?
  • Do you accept credit cards? / Can I pay in cash?

Making Plans

  • Do you want to…? / Let’s…
  • What time? / Where shall we meet?
  • See you later / See you soon

Expressing Opinions

  • I think… / I feel… / In my opinion…
  • I agree / I disagree
  • That sounds good / That doesn’t make sense
