LLM and NLP

LLM and NLP are related but not the same thing.

LLM and NLP

  • NLP = Natural Language Processing

    A broad field in AI focused on helping computers understand, process, and generate human language.

  • LLM = Large Language Model

    A type of AI model trained on massive amounts of text data to generate and understand language.

Examples of LLMs:

  • ChatGPT
  • Gemini
  • Claude

Relationship:

  • NLP is the field
  • LLMs are one of the technologies/models used in NLP

Examples of NLP tasks:

  • Translation
  • Sentiment analysis
  • Chatbots
  • Text summarization
  • Speech-to-text
  • Named entity recognition

Modern apps often use LLMs to perform NLP tasks more effectively.
