Managed Services

Managed services refers to a business model where a company outsources ongoing IT or operational responsibilities to a third-party provider called a Managed Service Provider (MSP). Instead of handling everything internally, the company pays the MSP to proactively manage, monitor, and support certain systems, infrastructure, or processes.

Depending on the MSP, services can include:

1. IT Infrastructure Management

  • Cost optimization

3. Cybersecurity Services

  • Firewall + endpoint protection
  • Threat monitoring (SOC)
  • Vulnerability scans
  • Incident response

4. Application Management

  • Deployment & updates
  • Bug monitoring
  • Performance optimization
  • Uptime SLAs

5. Helpdesk / Technical Support

  • User support (L1/L2/L3)
  • Device management
  • Ticketing & troubleshooting

6. Monitoring & Analytics

  • 24/7 monitoring
  • Automated alerts
  • Reporting for performance, usage, threats
