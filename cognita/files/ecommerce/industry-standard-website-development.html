  • Features:
    • Databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB).

    • Authentication & authorization.
    • Security best practices (HTTPS, OWASP Top 10 protections).

  • Content Management: WordPress, Strapi, Contentful.
  • E-Commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento.

F. Deployment & Hosting

  • Options: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Vercel, Netlify.
  • Best Practices:
    • Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD).
    • Version control with Git.
    • Staging environment before production.
    • SSL, backups, monitoring, and logging.

G. Maintenance & Updates

  • Security patches.
  • Performance optimization.
  • Content updates.
  • Analytics and A/B testing.
  • Industry standard: Monthly maintenance agreements for professional agencies.
