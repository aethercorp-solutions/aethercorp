Company Intranet Portal

Creating a company intranet portal can significantly improve internal communication, collaboration, and resource sharing within an organization. Here's a high-level overview to get you started, including key features, technology stack suggestions, and best practices.

üîß What Is a Company Intranet Portal?

A company intranet portal is a private, secure network accessible only to a company's employees. It acts as a central hub for sharing information, company news, resources, tools, and communication.

‚úÖ Core Features to Include

  1. Employee Directory
    • Profiles with photos, roles, departments, contact info
    • Searchable by name, department, skill, or location
  2. Company News & Announcements
    • CEO messages
    • HR updates
    • Upcoming events and holidays
  3. Document Management
    • Central repository for HR documents, policies, SOPs
    • Version control and access permissions
  4. Self-Service Tools
    • Leave application
    • Expense reporting
    • IT service requests
  5. Collaboration Spaces
    • Department or team pages
    • Project workspaces
    • Forums or message boards
  6. Search Functionality
    • Global search to find people, documents, or pages quickly
  7. Calendar Integration
    • Company-wide events
    • Department meetings
    • Holiday calendar
  8. Integrations
    • HRMS (e.g., BambooHR, Workday)
    • Slack or Teams
    • Google Workspace or Microsoft 365
    • CRM or internal tools
  9. Mobile Friendly or App Access
    • Responsive design or dedicated mobile apps for on-the-go access
