Natural Language Understanding (NLU)

Natural Language Understanding (NLU) is a subfield of Natural Language Processing (NLP) that focuses on enabling machines to understand and interpret human language in a meaningful way.

To understand language, NLU systems often rely on:

  • Machine Learning / Deep Learning
  • Large Language Models (LLMs) like GPT, BERT
  • Linguistic Rules (in older systems)
  • Knowledge Graphs or ontologies (for context and disambiguation)
