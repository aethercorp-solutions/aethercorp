Official requirements to enroll in the Apple Developer Program (2026)

Here are the official requirements to enroll in the Apple Developer Program (2026)—both for individuals and organizations.

🍎 Apple Developer Program Requirements

👤 1. Individual Account

This is the most common (what you likely need for apps).

✅ Basic requirements

  • Apple ID
    • Must have Two-Factor Authentication enabled
  • Age
    • At least 18 years old
  • Legal name
    • Must match your government ID
  • Payment method
    • Ability to pay the $99/year fee
  • Device
    • iPhone/iPad/Mac for enrollment (sometimes required for verification)

✅ Verification

  • Apple may require:
    • Government-issued ID
    • Face verification (via Apple Developer app)
  • Approval can take:
    • A few hours → several days

🏢 2. Organization (Company Account)

✅ Requirements

  • Must be a legal entity (corporation, LLC, etc.)
  • Provide:
    • D-U-N-S Number (company identity verification)
    • Legal company name & address
  • You must be:
    • Owner / founder / authorized employee
  • Company must be able to:
    • Sign legal agreements

✅ Additional verification

  • Apple may:
    • Call your company
    • Verify business registration

🏢 3. Enterprise Program (for internal apps only)

(Only if you distribute apps internally—not via App Store)

Requirements:

  • At least 100 employees
  • Must be a registered legal organization
  • Apps must be internal-use only
  • Must pass Apple’s verification process

💻 4. Technical Requirements (2026)

To submit apps:

  • Must use:
    • Xcode 26 or later
    • Latest SDKs (iOS 26, etc.)

💰 Cost

  • $99/year (Individual & Organization)
  • Enterprise: $299/year

⚠️ Common Reasons for Rejection (Important)

From real-world cases:

  • Apple ID issues (region mismatch, incomplete profile)
  • Using VPN or different country during enrollment
  • Invalid payment method
  • Failed identity verification

Example from developers:

“Enrollment couldn’t be completed… no explanation” (common issue reported)

💡 Pro Tips (especially for PH developers)

  • Use a PH-based Apple ID + billing address
  • Avoid VPN during signup
  • Enroll using the Apple Developer app (iPhone) — faster approval
  • Make sure your name matches your ID exactly

👍 Quick Checklist

  • Apple ID (2FA enabled)
  • Valid ID / identity verification ready
  • $99 payment method
  • Correct country + address
  • (Company) D-U-N-S number
