Here are the main iOS frameworks for video computing, grouped by use case. These are the official Apple frameworks plus the key tools widely used in iOS video apps.

üì± 1. Core iOS Video Frameworks

üîµ AVFoundation (Primary Video Framework)

The MOST important framework for any video work on iOS.

You can use it for:

  • Recording video (AVCaptureSession, AVCaptureVideoDataOutput)
  • Playing video (AVPlayer)
  • Editing (AVAsset, AVMutableComposition)
  • Exporting and transcoding (AVAssetExportSession)
  • Reading/writing metadata
  • Hardware-accelerated encoding/decoding (VideoToolbox integration)

This is the heart of video computing on iOS.

‚öôÔ∏è 2. Video Encoding / Decoding Frameworks

üîµ VideoToolbox

Low-level hardware-accelerated video codec framework.

Use when you need:

  • Real-time encoding (H.264, H.265)
  • Real-time decoding for streaming
  • Custom video pipelines (like WebRTC or AI apps)

Many apps like Zoom, TikTok, and WebRTC rely on this.
